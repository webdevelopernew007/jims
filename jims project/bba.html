Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

BBBA COURSE OFFERED BY JIMS IS AFFILIATED TO GURU GOBIND SINGH INDRAPRASTHA UNIVERSITY, DELHI

The programme is divided in six semesters. An academic year shall be apportioned into two semesters. Each of the two semesters shall be of working duration of about 21 weeks as per the following breakup.

  •         Imparting of Teaching 15 Weeks
  •         Preparatory leave about 01 weeks
  •         Semester end examination (including Practical) about 05 weeks

    There is a break of 2 weeks after every odd semesters and vacation of approximately 6 weeks after even semesters.

    PROGRAMME DURATION

    The minimum period required for completion of programme is 3 years(6 semesters)

    COURSE OFFERED AT

    Jagan Institute of Management Studies, 3, Institutional Area, Sector - 5, Rohini, Delhi-110085.

    ELIGIBILITY

    *Aggregate of 50% marks in the12th class for the purpose of eligibility will be taken as the aggregate of best four subjects (unless otherwise specified) including English and compulsory subject(s), if any.

    SELECTION CRITERIA

    Students must appear in Common Entrance Test conducted by GGSIPU. Only CET qualified applicants shall be considered for admission, through University Counselling subject to fulfilment of eligibility and admission criteria.

    COURSE OBJECTIVE

    Basic concepts of IT are imparted to the students. BCA provides understanding and skills related to the use of computer and its applications. The basic objective of the programme is to open a channel of admission for computing courses for students, who have done 10+2 and are interested in taking computing as a career.

    •         Total No of credits for BCA 3 Years Programme -140
    •         Total No of credits required for BCA 3 Years Programme -132

    PROGRAMME HIGHLIGHTS

    At Jagan Institute of Management Studies, faculty is chosen with great care keeping in mind the diversity of the programme. Ample computer time is made available to the students in the well-equipped computer labs to do practical work. The distinguishing feature of BBA programme at JIMS is state of the art physical infrastructure, experienced faculty drawn from academics and industry, emphasis on live projects, corporate exposure, entrepreneurial incubation, Wi Fi enabled internet access on campus. We provide you a platform to explore endless opportunities for stepping into further studies and research.
